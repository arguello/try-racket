Before we do that, let's think about the strategy behind this simple game. The basic steps are:

  • set the upper and lower limits of the player's number
  • guess a number halfway between the upper and lower limits
  • lower the upper limit if the player says the number is smaller
  • raise the lower limit if the player says the number is bigger

I'll let you in on a secret: your program won't actually guess the number, it will find it using a simple and effective binary search algorithm.
