That was easy, right?

Coding is often about breaking a task into small pieces, and putting those pieces together to form a program.

As you noticed, we listed the basic steps necessary for the Guess My Number game. We then defined the variables and functions to represent those steps.

Another thing that made this easy was our programming language of choice: Racket

Racket is an immensely powerful language - we call it a multi-paradigm language because it supports multiple styles of computer programming.

It is also a programmable programming language, meaning you can customize it to your domain or preferred style.

At the same time, Racket was designed in a way that makes programming fun and easy to learn.
